1.1 Business Name: Hamba LTD  

Organisation Description: 

Hamba Ltd is a non-government organisation (NGO) that focuses on improving community development and promoting sustainability's. Our platform is design to help local communities with essential services, opportunities and so much more. We intend to bridge the gap between urban settlement and rural areas. Our main aim is to combat poverty especially in rural areas. We also raise awareness about environment issues and encourage people   to take part in sustainable practices. The organisation aims to support local communities by providing information, resources and opportunities for growth.  

We have helped numerous people across the country as it parts our daily motivation. For instance, Durban weather is one of the most extreme when it comes to rainfalls. There are extreme heavy rainfalls and floods that affects the communities, infrastructure, and the environment. 

As Hamba LTD we play an essential role in helping these communities cope with these challenges. We provide emergency relief such as food, clean water, clothing, and temporary shelter during these disasters.  

We offer comfort and support to the vulnerable groups and assist in building shelter and repairing of infrastructure. Hamba LTD also specialises in planting of trees and renewing of drainage systems and promoting sustainable living practices to reduce the impact of extreme weather.
